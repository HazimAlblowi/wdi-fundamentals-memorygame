Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmansim, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are filpped face up over each turn. The objective of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.
